Description

Let's make a Mediterranean Air Fryer Chickenn Souvlaki Board. This is the perfect meal for someone who has had to change their diet due to a heart attack.

For the Air Fryer Chicken Souvlaki:-Wooden Skewers, soaked in water if time allows.

    -1 1/2 - 2lbs. Chicken Breasts, boneless and skinless
    -2 tbsp. Olive Oil
    -1 tbsp. Lemon Zest
    -1 tbsp. Apple Cider Vinegar
    -1 tbsp. Garlic, freshly minced or dried granules
    -1 tbsp. Italian Herb Seasoning
    -1 tbsp. Paprika
    -1 tsp. Ground Cumin
    -1 tsp. Salt

For the Pita Pockets / Mediterranean Board

    -Organic Baby Spinach
    -Pita Bread, halved
    -Olives
    -Crumbled Feta
    -Pickled Onions
    -Tzatziki & Hummus
    -Fresh Herbs and/or Microgreens
    -Cherry Tomatoes, quartered
    -Baby Cucumbers, diced

Instructions:

  1. Slice the chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces
  2. In a large bowl, combine the chopped chicken, olive oil, lemon zest, apple cider vinegar, and seasonings. Mix well. If time allows, let marinate in the fridge for 20-30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the Air Fryer at 375F for 3 minutes.
  4. Thread the seasoned chicken onto the wooden skewers. Add the skewers to the air fryer to cook for 10-15 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden brown and fully cooked through.
  5. Enjoy the chicken souvlaki by making pita pockets with the baby spinach, hummus, tzatziki, cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, and so on!
